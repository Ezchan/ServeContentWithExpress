About

Hi, my name is Eric Channey. I am a senior in college at Michigan State University. I began website development at the age of 12 when I built my first website Arcadequest. From there I went on to build small programs and more websites throughout highschool. After I ran out of projects, I went on to launch my first company Arkeni Solutions.

After running my business for a while I noticed that alot of people needed web hosting to go with their web development, so I launched Arkeni Hosting. After more time spent developing websites, I decided I wanted to delve further into software development. To do this I had to stop doing work for my own company due to lack of work and decided to join a local software company. Joining this software company at only 19, and being able to work on multiple projects there was very keen in my programming experience development. Some languages I like to work with are Python, C#, and JavaScript. To find out about some of the technologies and projects I've worked on visit the Work tab via navigation above.
